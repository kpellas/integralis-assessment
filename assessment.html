Established Capability Pillars

ITSM & Service Management Maturity

67% Established

ITSM practices are mature and well-governed. Opportunities remain in automation, optimisation, and proactive service management.

Strengths Identified

  • Service desk processes are well-documented and followed consistently across the organisation.
  • Incidents follow a structured and reliable triage and resolution process.
  • Changes follow a documented and communicated change management workflow with appropriate approvals.

Operational Excellence & Intelligent Automation

Strengths Identified

  • Monitoring and alerting provide strong visibility across systems with proactive issue detection.
  • Operations follow clearly documented standard procedures.
  • Routine operational tasks are automated, improving efficiency and consistency.

Optimisation Opportunities

  • Intelligent automation opportunities remain unimplemented, leading to preventable manual work.
  • Implement event correlation and automated RCA insights.
  • Optimise automation coverage and remove remaining manual steps.

90-Day Improvement Roadmap

Focus on your two weakest pillars (Cyber and Technical Capability) to achieve measurable risk reduction within 90 days:

Phase 1: Days 0-30 (Stabilise)

  • Deploy MFA enforcement across all users
  • Establish monthly patching schedule
  • Review admin/privileged access accounts
  • Validate backup integrity and test restore
  • Implement centralised device management
  • Document identity lifecycle processes

Phase 2: Days 30-90 (Improve)

  • Deploy EDR/XDR across all endpoints
  • Implement basic network segmentation
  • Configure centralised logging and monitoring
  • Document incident response procedures
  • Establish security baseline enforcement
  • Test DR procedures quarterly
